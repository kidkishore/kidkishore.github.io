My Projects


VR Protein Databank Visualizer



A virtual reality protein visualizer which realizes proteins parsed from the Nation Protein Databank. The software VMD (Visual Molecular Dynamics) was used to parse the .pdb files and create different .obj representations of the proteins. Unity was then used to add laser point interactivity, including moving, rotation, and scaling the different protein models and representations.

Virtual India Tour



During my 3 week vacation in India the Winter of 2017, I captured several dozen 360 photos of Indian sights and monuments with the Rico Theta S camera. I compiled some of the best into this Virtual Reality, point-and-click tour of India, featuring photospheres of the different photos I took. App was built for the HTC Vive.


Galaxy Balls VR - CS 498 Final Project



My final project for University of Illinois' Virtual Reality class, CS 498. Made with one other group member, the game is a 3D space-themed billiards-style game built for the Oculus Rift.


Dental Dog VR Prototype: University of Illinois College of Veterinary Medicine



A prototype of an interactable 3D model of a Dog Skull in VR, built for the University of Illinois College of Veterinary Medicine. Built to aid student learning in dog teeth extraction.
